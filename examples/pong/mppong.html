Project Summary

The aim of this study is to examine people's strategy in a multi-player coordination game.

Time Commitment

The study typically takes 20–25 minutes to complete.

Benefits & Risks

There are no direct benefits or risks from your participation.

Eligibility Requirements

You can participate only once. United States citizen/resident. 18 years or older. English speaker.

You will receive $5.00 US for your participation. If your data are below a minimal threshold for quality, you will not be compensated.

All research data will be stored securely and confidentially. Identifying details will not be associated with any data provided.
